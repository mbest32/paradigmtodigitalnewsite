Why Work With Us?

Job Placement Done Right

1

Employment Alignment

The power of alignment recruiting is designed to benefit both the company as well as the employee.  Though this is optional, you will have the ability to go through this process to not only learn about yourself, but also how to deal with managers and coworkers that may communicate and think totally different from you.  The data gathered from this process can also be used to help you discover which type of work cultures and managers you may potentially have the best connections with. It will also help you to understand how to deal with those that you work with as a contractor, should you choose to become a freelancer.

Global Opportunities

The world is changing at a dramatic pace.  It has also become a global market place with vast opportunities.  The US alone accounts for 57 million of the 75 million people across the globe that work in the gig economy. With new opportunites comes more competition.  With paradigmtodigital we work to connect employers from arround the world with talent from around the world. As an employee or Freelancer this can offer you a distinct advantage.
